Presentation & Demo

Turn the confirmed value argument into a storyboard for the real product demonstration.

The idea

The demo earns the trust

The slides make the argument; the live demo earns the trust, and together they tell one story.

  • The customer presentation is built in the official Kärcher Slides template; this step's AI work is the demo storyboard that proves your value argument.
  • A demo is a sequence of proofs, not a feature tour: each step ties to a confirmed need, with a short talk-track line and the likely objection ready.
