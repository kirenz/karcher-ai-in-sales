Presentation & Demo

Turn the confirmed value argument into a lean role-aware presentation and a real demonstration storyboard.

The idea

One thread: slides and demo

The slides make the argument; the live demo earns the trust, and together they tell one story.

  • Turn your value block from Step 6 into a lean three-slide story (situation, fit, decision), one idea per slide, plus a storyboard for the live demo.
  • Every claim on a slide should have a matching proof in the demo, so the two tell one story instead of a pitch and a separate show.
