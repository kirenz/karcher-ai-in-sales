Acquisition

Use AI to become the visible expert in your industry, so customers come to you. Or use AI to find the accounts worth approaching.

The idea

Two roads to a lead

Two roads to a lead: inbound (be found as an expert) and outbound (source a shortlist). Both feed Step 2.

  • Acquisition creates a lead where none exists yet. Two roads: inbound (be found) and outbound (go find them).
  • Inbound is social selling: expert positioning, not a cold pitch, give useful industry insight first, then get asked.
