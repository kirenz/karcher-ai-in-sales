Deep Research

A mode in Gemini that researches the web, or your own Gmail and Drive, before it answers.

What it is

A research analyst, not an instant answer

Deep Research is a mode inside Gemini, not a separate tool. Instead of answering in one shot, it plans, runs many searches, reads the sources it finds, and writes a structured, cited report.

It takes minutes, not seconds, and works in the background while you do something else.

  • Switch it on yourself in a Gemini chat, or find it already on inside some prepared Gems.
  • Pick your sources: the public web, or your own Gmail and Drive.
  • It can sound confident and still be wrong, so click the citations behind claims that matter.

Do it yourself

Run your own Deep Research

You don't have to wait for a prepared Gem. In any Gemini chat you can run one yourself:

  1. 1

    Click the + at the bottom-left of the chat and pick Deep Research from the menu.

  2. 2

    Open Sources and choose where it looks: the web, your Gmail, your Drive, or Chat.

  3. 3

    Type your research question and send it.

    Example question

    Research the logistics and warehousing sector in Germany: its main cost and operational pressures, and where cleaning, hygiene, and machine uptime matter. Cite recent, primary sources.

  4. 4

    It shows a research plan first, your chance to steer it. Click Start research if it fits, or Edit plan and say what to change.

  5. 5

    Give it a few minutes; it comes back with a structured, cited report.

  6. 6

    When it's done, export the report as a Google Doc (the option is at the top) so you can reuse it or feed it to another Gem.
