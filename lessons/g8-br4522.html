Our example product, and the deal we work at NorthStar.

What it is

One product, worked all the way through

The BR 45/22 is a Kärcher battery scrubber drier, a walk-behind machine that scrubs and dries hard floors. It's the product at the heart of the running example.

NorthStar runs a mixed, aging fleet and machines break down mid-shift, so the opportunity is a pilot of three BR 45/22 units. A broader rollout opens only after the pilot proves out.

  • It stands in for whatever you actually sell, the method is the same for any product.
  • The deal is a pilot first: three machines, then a broader rollout only if it proves out.
  • Examples never quote real prices. Internal files like the YelloFile are fine to attach in approved Workspace tools; just keep actual prices out of anything the customer sees.
