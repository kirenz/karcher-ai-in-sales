Value Argumentation

Turn the confirmed need profile into a customer-specific value argument.

The idea

Start from the need

Start from the need, not the catalogue: every benefit has to trace back to something the customer actually said they need.

  • Take the needs the customer confirmed in Step 5 and turn each one into a clear reason this offer is worth it for them, that is your value argument.
  • Don't stop at what the machine has; the value is in what it does and why that matters to this customer, so spend your effort there, then verify every figure.
