Data safety & the quality gate

What you may put in, and the check before anything reaches a customer.

What it is

Green light in approved tools, with a check

In approved Kärcher Workspace tools (in this training: Gemini with Gems, Deep Research, and NotebookLM), signed in with your Kärcher account, you may work with real customer data: the contract keeps it confidential, and the tools never reuse it or learn from it.

Your part is light but real: share only what the task needs, then verify the output before it reaches the customer.

  • Before anything reaches a customer, run the two-step gate: a good prompt in, then validate the output.
  • Validate means checking each claim against its source and cutting what you can't back up, no evidence, no send.
  • Formal proposals, pricing, legal and contract terms still go through the normal sign-off.
