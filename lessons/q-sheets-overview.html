Kärcher · Quick help

See your pipeline at a glance

One prompt turns your account list into a compact overview: value by stage and by segment.

You have

A Google Sheet with your accounts — or the ready-made practice tracker.

You’ll create

A pivot table on its own new tab: your pipeline value by stage and by segment.

Time

About 5 minutes, right inside Sheets.

What you need

Just the sheet

Your sheetA table with your accounts: one row per account, columns like stage, segment, value.

No sheet of your own yet?

Use the practice tracker (File → Make a copy) — it has stages, segments and values ready to summarise.

Open the practice tracker ↗

Do it · in Sheets

One prompt, one new tab

Gemini creates the summary on a new tab by itself — nothing to insert, nothing to place. If it shows a small plan with an Approve button first, just approve it.

Copy-paste prompt:

What is my total pipeline value by stage and by segment? Give me the result as a new table in a new tab called pipeline value. Use only what is in my data.

  1. 1

    Open Ask Gemini (top right) and run the prompt.

  2. 2

    Open the new "pipeline value" tab at the bottom — your pivot table is there.

Open Google Sheets ↗

Check it

Trust it, then verify one total

An overview is only useful if the numbers hold. Thirty seconds:

  • Pick one small segment and add its values by hand — does the total match?
  • Read the assumptions it states (excluded rows, currencies) and confirm the ones that matter.
  • The table says where the money sits — what you do about it stays your call.

Done

You produced: A one-glance overview of your pipeline, straight from your own sheet.
