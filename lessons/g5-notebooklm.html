NotebookLM

An expert that answers only from the sources you gave it.

What it is

Answers from your sources, with citations

NotebookLM is the expert who has read exactly the sources you loaded, and shows its source for every answer.

Ask a plain question and the answer comes only from those sources, never from general knowledge. Every answer carries numbered citations you click to check the exact passage.

  • It tells you when the sources do not cover something, instead of guessing.
  • It can also summarise, draft a briefing, or build a table from the same sources.
