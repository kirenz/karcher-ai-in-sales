The idea

Confident is not verified

BANT is a hypothesis from public information until confirmed in conversation. Confident is not the same as verified.

  • A raw lead is only a name; this step turns it into a sourced briefing and an honest Go or No-Go, so your time flows to the accounts that earn it.
  • A prepared Gem with Deep Research does the finding and returns a structured eight-part briefing; your effort goes to judgment, not gathering.
