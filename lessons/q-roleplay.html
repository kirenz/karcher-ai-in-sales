Kärcher · Quick help

Test & simulate your own customer negotiation

Practice & strengthen your self-confidence.

You have

An upcoming customer conversation or negotiation you want to walk into prepared.

You’ll create

A realistic practice conversation that challenges your sales skills and your product and cleaning expertise.

Time

About 5 minutes, in Gemini — longer if the negotiation gets tough.

What you need

You and a scenario

Introduce yourselfWho you are and your role — in your native language. The Gem is multilingual.
The conversation to practiceThe situation you want to rehearse: the customer, the setting, and what makes it tricky.

Do it · with the Gem

Start the role-play

The Kärcher Sales Role-Play Simulator acts as your practice partner for sales conversations. Based on the Kärcher training know-how, it challenges your sales skills and your product and cleaning expertise.

Open the Gem, introduce yourself in your native language, and describe the conversation you want to practice.

Copy-paste prompt:

Hi, I'm [your name], [your role] at Kärcher. I want to practice [the conversation — e.g. a price negotiation with a purchasing manager].

Open the Sales Role-Play Simulator ↗

Check it

After the role-play

The practice round is raw material for the real one:

  • Ask the Gem for feedback: what worked, and where did it have to go easy on you?
  • Note the one objection that felt hardest — that is the one to prepare properly.
  • Say your best answers out loud once; saying it is different from reading it.

Done

✓

Task done

You produced: A practice negotiation behind you — and your hardest objection identified before the real one.
