Kärcher Sales Cycle

Contacts & First Contact

Turn the research brief into a stakeholder map, then write one first email built to win the meeting, not close the deal.

The idea

Match the role, not a personality

Every stakeholder entry is a hypothesis to verify. Match the first message to the role, not to an invented personality.

  • In B2B you sell to a Buying Center: several people who each judge the same offer by a different question (outcome, cost and risk, daily work).
  • Map the stakeholders before you write, straight from the research brief, every entry a hypothesis to verify, never an invented person.
