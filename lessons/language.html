Read it in your language

The platform is written in English, but your browser can translate every page into your language in one click. Here is how, in Google Chrome.

Deutsch In Ihrer Sprache lesen · Français Lire dans votre langue · Español Léelo en tu idioma · Italiano Leggi nella tua lingua · Nederlands Lees in je eigen taal · Polski Czytaj w swoim języku · Português Leia no seu idioma · 中文 用您的语言阅读

1

Right-click anywhere on the page

In the menu that appears, choose Translate to Deutsch (or your own language). In Microsoft Edge it works the same way.

2

Not your language? Pick it once

Click the small translate icon in the address bar (top right), then ⋮ → Choose another language. Chrome remembers your choice.

3

The whole platform, in your language

Every page now reads in your language, automatically. Only the spoken Listen audio stays in English.

On a phone: in the Chrome app, tap the ⋮ menu, then Translate.

Back to English: click the translate icon in the address bar, then Show original.

Why not ready-made translations? One English version keeps the platform easy to keep up to date — every fix reaches everyone at once. Full translations may follow later if the need is there.
