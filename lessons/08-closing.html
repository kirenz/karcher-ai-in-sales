Closing

Read the buying signal, choose the right path, answer objections calmly, and capture the result.

The idea

The right next step, at the right moment

Closing is choosing the right next step at the right moment.

  • The close is three moves: notice when the customer is ready to move, choose your next step, and write down what was agreed.
  • Match how directly you ask to how ready they seem: a light 'what if' when the signal is soft, a clear ask once scope and timing are settled.
