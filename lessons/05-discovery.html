Needs Analysis

Understand what they need. Walk into the conversation with sharp questions, and walk out with a clear picture of what the customer actually needs.

The idea

Prepare the conversation

This step is about getting ready for the conversation. You prepare the questions that get the customer talking about what they really need, not questions that pitch your solution. The AI drafts them; you choose the ones you will actually ask.

  • Good questions come first. You go in with a short, sharp set that gets the customer talking about what they need, before you propose anything.
  • The AI drafts the questions; you decide which to ask. It stays your conversation, not the tool's script.
