The full introduction

Five ways to use the platform, explained in about three minutes — read it, or let your guide talk you through.

Hello, and a very warm welcome. This is AI in Sales — your everyday companion for making artificial intelligence work in your daily sales business.

The platform is built on a simple idea: there is no one-size-fits-all way to learn. Depending on what you need right now in your working day, you choose how to use it. There are five ways in.

1Quick help

Your instant, task-based assistant

If you are busy and need to get a specific task done right now, Quick help is the fastest door — and the most prominent one. It is built on a survey of 75 sales colleagues and focuses on the top priorities named there: data analysis, meeting preparation and follow-up, emails, and translations. You will find seventeen task cards, such as “Reply to a customer email”, “Find insights in a sales sheet”, or “Get ready for a customer meeting”. Each card takes about five minutes: open it, copy the ready-made prompt, and get your actual work done — no preparation, no case study.

Open Quick help →

2AI Basics

Build your foundations

Curious how the new tools actually work? AI Basics builds your foundations in seven short, interactive lessons: what AI actually is, how to brief it, Gemini and Gems, Deep Research, NotebookLM, Workspace Studio, and the data rules. The right place to build confidence before — or alongside — your daily work.

Start with the basics →

3Sales Cycle

Learn by example

If you want to see how AI fits into your sales process as a whole, take the Sales Cycle course: nine steps from lead to follow-up, worked end to end on one consistent example customer — NorthStar, a fictional facility-services company. You follow the journey from start to finish, with example Gem outputs and ready-made documents at every step.

Start the course →

4Guide

The complete reference handbook

Need to look up a detail, a guideline, or what a tool can do? The Guide is the complete handbook behind everything. Read it end to end, or use it as your reference whenever you need the depth behind a step.

Open the guide →

5Knowledge Base

Ask instead of searching

And if you prefer asking to searching: the Knowledge Base is the same handbook as a notebook. Ask a question in your own words and get an answer with citations from the guide.

Ask the notebook →

Why this structure?

Why this structure? Because learning a new skill and getting help in the flow of work are two different needs. Under a tight deadline, Quick help saves your day in five minutes. And once you see how easy it is, the basics and the course are there whenever you have the time to go deeper.

So, how would you like to start today? Polish a presentation, prepare for a meeting — or start with the basics? The platform is ready for you. Click around and explore.
