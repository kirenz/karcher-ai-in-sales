Kärcher · Quick help

Improve an existing presentation

One click rebuilds a cluttered slide. Your message stays yours.

You have

A deck that does the job but looks rough: crowded slides, topic-label titles. Or the practice deck.

You’ll create

A cleaner, better-structured version of the slide, in your deck's own brand and style.

Time

About 5 minutes, right inside Google Slides.

What you need

A deck and its message

Your deckThe presentation open in Google Slides — or your own copy of the practice deck.
Your messageWhat the slide should say. Gemini restructures; the message stays yours.

No deck to improve right now?

Use the practice deck: a short, fictitious sales update whose slides are deliberately rough — the ten-bullet feature slide is your raw material. Open it, then File → Make a copy, and improve your copy.

Open the practice deck ↗

Do it · in Slides

One click: Enhance this slide

Select the slide that needs work, click Ask Gemini (top right), and choose Enhance this slide. Gemini analyzes your deck and proposes a rebuilt version — in your deck's brand, colors, and fonts, and fully editable afterwards (a normal slide, not an image). Not happy? Run it again for another take.

Then hold the result against your original: the main message and your confirmed facts stay, repetition gets shorter, and nothing new appears.

No Enhance option in your panel? Ask for it in words instead:

Copy-paste prompt:

Rebuild this slide with a clearer structure. Keep the main message and all confirmed facts, shorten repetition, and do not add new content. Keep the deck's colors and fonts, and use only rectangular shapes — no rounded corners.

Open Google Slides ↗

Check it

The title states the answer, not the topic

Enhance makes a slide cleaner; it cannot decide what the slide says. Before you accept:

  • Are the main message and your confirmed facts still there — repetition shortened, nothing new added?
  • Does the title state the answer, not the topic? "Product overview" names a topic; a strong title tells the audience what to take away.
  • Kärcher brand check: graphic elements are rectangular. If Gemini added rounded corners, fix the shapes or run it again.
  • Still crowded? Run Enhance again, or split it: one idea per slide.

Done

✓

Task done

You produced: A slide you can present: same message, cleaner structure, your deck's own look.
