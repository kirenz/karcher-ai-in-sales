Workspace Studio

The tool for routines: describe a flow once, and it runs every time.

What it is

A standing flow, described in words

Most AI help is on demand: you ask, you get an answer, once. Workspace Studio is the other kind — it takes over routines that repeat. You describe the task once, in plain words, and Gemini builds a flow that from then on runs by itself: when something happens — an email arrives, a file in Drive changes, a set time comes — the flow does its step.

Every flow has three parts: a trigger ("when this happens…"), an action ("…do that"), and your decision at the end. A flow can notify you on its own — but what happens next stays your call.

  • You describe the whole flow in one prompt — Gemini builds the steps, you adjust them afterwards.
  • Automate after the habit, not before it: only a routine you already run cleanly by hand earns a flow.
