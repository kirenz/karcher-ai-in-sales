Turn your sheet into a short report

The numbers stay in the sheet; the story goes into an email. Gemini writes the story.

You have

A Google Sheet with your accounts or deals — or the practice tracker.

You’ll create

A short written report you can paste into an email: pipeline by stage, biggest open deals, accounts that need attention.

Time

About 5 minutes, right inside Sheets.

What you need

Just the sheet

Your sheetA table with your accounts: one row per account, columns like stage, value, last contact, next step.

No sheet of your own yet?

Use the practice tracker (File → Make a copy) — it has everything the report needs.

Open the practice tracker ↗

Do it · in Sheets

Ask for the story, not the table

Open your sheet, click Ask Gemini (top right), and run the prompt. You get plain text, ready to paste into an email or a Doc.

Copy-paste prompt:

Summarise this account sheet as a short status report I can paste into an email: total pipeline value by stage, the three biggest open deals, and the accounts with no recent contact or no next step. Use only what is in the sheet.

Open Google Sheets ↗

Check it

A report others act on

This text travels — check it before it does:

  • Verify one number by hand — the biggest deal or one stage total — before anyone quotes it.
  • Read the assumptions it states (like which date counts as "today") and correct what matters.
  • It is a snapshot of the sheet, not a forecast — say so when you send it.

Done

✓

Task done

You produced: A status report in plain words, grounded in your own sheet.
