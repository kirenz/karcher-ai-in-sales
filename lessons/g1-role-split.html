What AI is for

What AI takes off your plate, and what stays yours.

What it is

AI does the grunt work, you keep the judgment

Generative AI takes the repeatable preparation off your plate: research, summaries, first drafts.

What stays yours is the judgment, the trust, and the customer relationship.

  • AI is strongest when you need a fast, structured first draft.
  • It can also invent facts and figures that sound certain, miss your account context, or be out of date.
  • It supports you, it doesn't replace you: understanding the customer and building trust stay yours.
  • Trust the draft, verify what matters, then use it.
