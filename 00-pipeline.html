Chapter highlights

  • One weekly question: which of your accounts get your time this week?
  • The move has two steps: get your accounts into one Google Sheet, then point a Gem at it and ask, in plain words, where the week should go. No sheet yet? Build one in minutes, let NotebookLM assemble it from documents you already have.
  • Watch two fields above all, last contact and next step: an account missing both is not lost, just forgotten, and forgotten accounts quietly cost you deals.
  • AI sorts and surfaces; you decide. Your pipeline is real customer data in an approved tool, so analyze it directly, then check the ranking against the sheet before you act.
