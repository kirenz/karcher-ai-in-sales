Chapter highlights

  • The shortest step, on purpose: AI sharpens your preparation, the conversation stays human.
  • Prepare three things before you walk in, confidence, conviction, context. AI helps you prepare all three.
  • Use DISC as an observation framework to read the person live and adapt your opening, pace, and detail; the type is a hypothesis read in the room, never AI-profiled from a public footprint.
  • Prepared awareness, never scripted warmth: stop at the hand-off line, where the tool ends and you listen.
